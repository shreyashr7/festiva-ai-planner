BENGALURU VENUE DIRECTORY (2026)
North Bengaluru (Hebbal/Yelahanka):

The King's Meadows: A premium lush green outdoor venue ideal for large-scale weddings and product launches. Known for its Balinese architecture and vast lawns.

Manpho Convention Centre: A massive multi-hall facility near Manyata Tech Park, perfect for corporate exhibitions and large traditional weddings.

Vara The Pavilion: A serene, upscale venue in Yelahanka suitable for niche corporate retreats and intimate wedding ceremonies.

East Bengaluru (Whitefield/Marathahalli):

Rustique Winds: A specialized corporate event space that blends a rustic aesthetic with high-tech AV infrastructure. Preferred by tech firms for off-sites.

Sheraton Grand Whitefield: A luxury hotel providing ballroom spaces for high-end corporate galas and international conferences.

The Zuri: Known for its poolside events and sophisticated indoor halls, popular for both corporate networking and cocktail dinners.

Central & South Bengaluru (Indiranagar/JP Nagar):

Royal Orchid (Old Airport Road): A landmark for corporate award ceremonies and rooftop sundowners.

Gowri Kalyana Mantapa (Basavanagudi): A traditional heritage hall perfect for South Indian weddings, known for its classic architecture.

The Black Rabbit (Indiranagar): Often utilized for small-scale social mixers and creative brand launches.

PLANNING TIMELINES & LOGISTICS
Booking Windows: For peak wedding seasons (Nov-Feb), popular venues in Hebbal and Whitefield require a minimum of 4-6 months lead time. Corporate events usually book 4-8 weeks in advance.

The 6-Week Implementation Cycle:

Week 1-2: Venue finalization, vendor onboarding, and budget locking.

Week 3-4: Menu tasting, decor design approval, and guest list management.

Week 5: Technical rehearsals, AV checks, and final vendor walkthroughs.

Week 6: Event execution and post-event settlement.

Bengaluru Traffic Strategy: Planners are advised to schedule event starts at 11:00 AM or 7:30 PM to avoid the peak Silk Board and Marathahalli rush. Always provide shuttle services for venues in North Bengaluru if the guest base is in the South.
